## About LegacyWorks Group

At LegacyWorks, we partner with changemakers—funders, nonprofits, impact investors, social enterprises, and public agencies—to move big ideas into action. Through tailored engagements, we help design and scale solutions that align community priorities with environmental, economic, and social outcomes.

Our team brings deep experience in strategy, facilitation, communications, and collaborative design. Whether it’s developing a multi-million-dollar fund, launching a regional initiative, or navigating cross-sector relationships, we work alongside clients and partners to build the infrastructure and momentum for lasting change.
